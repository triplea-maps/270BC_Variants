Created by: Doctor Che


A mod by Cernel in which everything is cut to about 1/3

Specifically:
- All productions are cut to about 1/3
- All starting units are cut to about 1/3
- The cost of the city is reduced from 15 to 5
- City cannot be in value 0 territory

More Specifically:
- The productions are so reduced, per territory:
Old : New
0,1 : 0
2,3,4 : 1
5,6,7 : 2
8+ : 3

- The starting units are so reduced, per territory:
0: Reduced the total number of units per territory same values as production above
Exceptions1: Single bireme are kept and single trireme are substituted with bireme
Exceptions2: Single neutral units are substituted with cavalry
1: Kept the greatest number of different units
2: Left the units present in the greatest numbers
3A: Left the most pricey units, if the new total number was integer or rounded down
3B: Left the cheapest units, if the new total number was rounded up
4AB: Left the better defence units, same cases as 3 above
Exceptions: For ships, the point 2 and 3 are inverted

After changes:
+1 production Emesa
+1 production Cirta
-1 warelephant Lilybaeum
+1 spearman Lilybaeum
-1 archer Alexandria
-1 chariot Jerusalem
+1 hoplite Seleucia
-1 cavalry Persepolis

Notes:
The mod keeps about the same gameplay dynamics as the original 1.5 (except that the round 1 attack in best defended Lilybaeum is not worth it, at about 40% success).


Make yourself an empire around the Mediterranean Ocean (the known world),
in the era when Hellenes, Romans and Phoenicians ruled. Choose from a arsenal of
legionaires, hoplites, onagers, cataphracts, triremes, warelephants and many more.
Territory names are based on cities at the time given or
take 500 years (not easy finding names for all).
